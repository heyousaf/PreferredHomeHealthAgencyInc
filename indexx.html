About Our Agency

Run by people who understand what's at stake.

Preferred Home Health Agency was built on a simple idea: the families trusting us with a parent or child deserve the same care, patience, and professionalism they'd give their own. Families across Miami-Dade have relied on our team — from short-term recovery support to multi-year, end-of-life care — and the relationships often outlast the assignment.

  • Carefully screened, experienced nurse aides and caregivers
  • Dedicated case managers who stay involved, not just on day one
  • Flexible scheduling, including 24/7 live-in and overnight coverage
  • Serving families throughout Miami-Dade County

What We Offer

Support for every stage of care

From a few hours of help a week to round-the-clock live-in support, our care plans are shaped around your family's needs — not a one-size-fits-all schedule.

Personal Care Aides

Help with bathing, dressing, mobility, and daily routines — delivered with patience and respect for dignity.

Skilled Nursing Visits

Licensed nurses for medication management, wound care, vitals monitoring, and post-hospital recovery.

Companion & Senior Care

Friendly, consistent companionship paired with light housekeeping, meal prep, and errand support.

24/7 & Live-In Care

Overnight, weekend, and full live-in coverage for families who need continuous peace of mind.

Post-Surgical Recovery

Short-term, intensive support to help patients heal safely at home after a hospital stay or procedure.

Pediatric & Family Care

Specialized in-home support for children with medical or developmental needs, alongside the whole family.
